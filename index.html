Crystal Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. But if your total score goes above the random number, then you lose.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Every time you win or lose, the game will generate a new random number for you to guess. The values of the crystals will also change with each new generation.
